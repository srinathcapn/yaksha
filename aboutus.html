We want to let you know more about us

Yaksha Travels, the sister company of Chetan Travels who is an major player in cab aggregate business from past 3 decades serving major government sectors including ISRO, NAL, HAL and many private companies. Yaksha travels, which is certified by Karnataka Tourism Development Board mainly focus on the the corporate and start-up sector.

We are aiming for a better and greener Bengaluru with corporate tie ups. We are using and promoting eco-friendly electric vehicles as our Social responsibility towards society.
